Welcome Message

On behalf of the organizing committee, I am very pleased to invite you to the 11thCongress of the Asian Pan-Pacific Society of Pediatric Gastroenterology, Hepatology and Nutrition (APPSPGHAN 2009) in Seoul, Korea on September 25-28, 2009. Following the last congress in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, we look forward to welcoming you to Seoul, Korea for a successful APPSPGHAN meeting in 2009.

Under the theme of “Tradition, Evolution and Harmony for Healthy Children”, APPSPGHAN2009 will facilitate among fellows and congress delegates, as many Pediatricians, Gastroenterologists, Hepatologists, Nutritionists and Public Health Specialists are expected to attend from all around the world. We look forward to meeting you in autumn in Seoul and believe APPSPGHAN2009 will be a memorable occasion for the fruitful interchange of ideas and the development of long-lasting friendship in the coming years.

Sincerely yours, Jeong-Kee Seo, M.D.
President of the APPSPGHAN2009
